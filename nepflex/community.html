Sharing my favorite post-workout smoothie recipe that helps with recovery:

- 1 banana
- 1 scoop protein powder
- 1 tbsp almond butter
- 1 cup almond milk
- Handful of spinach
- Ice cubes

Blend until smooth! The potassium from the banana helps with muscle cramps and the protein aids in recovery.
